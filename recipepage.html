Spicy Lemon Garlic Pasta

A bright, zesty weeknight pasta that comes together in under 25 minutes—silky, garlicky, and finished with a whisper of chili heat.

Serves: 2–3 Prep: 10 min Cook: 15 min Vegan-friendly

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti (or your favorite pasta)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 lemon (zest and juice)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in well-salted water until al dente. Reserve 3/4 cup pasta water; drain.
  2. Warm olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 60–90 seconds.
  3. Stir in red pepper flakes and lemon zest; cook 20 seconds.
  4. Add 1/2 cup reserved pasta water to the pan and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Toss in the drained pasta and lemon juice; swirl until the sauce becomes glossy, adding more water if needed.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper. Finish with parsley and serve immediately.
Tip: For extra richness, finish with a knob of butter or a drizzle of good olive oil. Share your spin with friends!
